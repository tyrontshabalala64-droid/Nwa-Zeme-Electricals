Why You Need a COC

In South Africa, a Certificate of Compliance (COC) is required by law when selling or renting a property. It confirms that the electrical installation is safe and meets SANS 10142-1 standards. Nwa Zeme Electricals is fully registered and qualified to issue COCs.
